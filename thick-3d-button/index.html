Thick 3D Button (1950s plunger feel)

Move your cursor over the button and press. Try keyboard too: Space / Enter.

How to use

  1. Copy thick-button.css and thick-button.js into your project.
  2. Use the markup: 
    <button class="btn3d" data-thickness="30" data-travel="22" data-tilt="10">
  <span class="btn3d__cap"><span class="btn3d__text">Label</span></span>
</button>
  3. Adjust data-thickness (height), data-travel (press depth), and data-tilt (hover tilt).
